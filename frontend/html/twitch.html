Twitch

If you are a Twitch streamer, you can connect a board to your Twitch chat. As the channel owner, use the command "!setboard [board_id]" in chat to specify which board to connect to. Audience members can use the command "!branch [start] [coordinates]" to add a branch to the tree. (For example, use "!branch 23 e17 d17 e16 c14" to add a branch starting at move 23).

Click here to authorize and subscribe the chatbot to your channel. You must be logged in to Twitch.

Click here to unsubscribe the chatbot from your channel. You must be logged in to Twitch.
